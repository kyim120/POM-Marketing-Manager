# **Marketing Manager – Work Done Report**

**Role:** Marketing Manager
**Startup:** *Trendify Clothing*
**Industry:** Online Clothing Brand
**Year:** 2026

---

### ✔ 1. **Researched Market Trends & Customer Behavior**

I conducted research on youth buying patterns in Pakistan.
Findings:

* Teenagers and young adults mostly shop through **Instagram, TikTok, and WhatsApp**.
* Trendy streetwear, oversized shirts, hoodies, and custom prints are highly popular.
* Customers prefer brands with **cool visuals, fast replies, and quick delivery**.

This helped me build an effective marketing strategy.

---

### ✔ 2. **Defined Target Customers Clearly**

I identified the ideal customers for Trendify Clothing PK:

#### **Primary Customers**

* Teenagers (13–19 years)
* University students (18–25 years)
* Young professionals (25–35 years)

#### **Customer Traits**

* Fashion-conscious
* Active on Instagram/TikTok
* Prefer affordable trendy clothing
* Love limited-edition drops

This shapes the brand image and marketing direction.

---

### ✔ 3. **Created the Competitive Advantage**

To stand out in a crowded clothing market, I finalized the following competitive advantages:

* Trendy and unique designs updated monthly
* Affordable pricing compared to top brands
* Customizable T-shirt & hoodie printing service
* Fast 2–3 day delivery
* Strong social media presence with Reels & influencers

These points make our brand appealing to youth.

---

### ✔ 4. **Developed the Main Marketing Strategy**

I built a complete marketing strategy focusing on social media visibility, brand building, and customer engagement.

#### **Marketing Strategy Highlights**

* Create **Instagram-first brand identity**
* Post daily stories + 3 feed posts per week
* Use **Reels/TikTok videos** to go viral
* Run ads targeting ages 15–30
* Use micro-influencers and college ambassadors
* Offer limited-edition clothing drops monthly

This ensures continuous engagement and brand growth.

---

### ✔ 5. **Planned the Launch Campaign**

I designed a strong launch campaign to attract the first 200–300 customers quickly.

#### **Launch Campaign Plan**

* **“30% OFF for first 200 customers”**
* Influencer Giveaway (2 influencers tagging our brand)
* Launch teaser posts 7 days before release
* Build hype using countdown stories

Goal: Achieve at least **500 followers** and **100 first-week orders**.

### ✔ 7. **Designed the Marketing Funnel**

I built a simple funnel to convert viewers into customers:

1. **Awareness:** Reels, TikTok, influencers
2. **Interest:** Instagram posts, stories
3. **Desire:** Limited-edition designs
4. **Action:** Discounts, call-to-action, fast delivery
5. **Retention:** WhatsApp offers + email reminders

This ensures repeat customers.
